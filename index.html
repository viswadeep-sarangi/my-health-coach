Workout split
Built around lower-body preference, triceps preference, shoulder comfort, mild DR, and home equipment.

Day 1

Lower body + core rehab

35–40 min
  • Glute bridges 4 × 12
  • Dumbbell Romanian deadlifts 3 × 10
  • Sit-to-stand squats 3 × 12
  • Banded lateral walks 3 × 15
  • Dead bug 3 × 10 each side

Day 2

Arms + back

30–35 min
  • One-arm dumbbell rows 3 × 10 each side
  • Banded rows 3 × 12
  • Tricep kickbacks 3 × 12
  • Hammer curls 3 × 12
  • Wall push-ups or incline push-ups 2 × 10

Day 3

Lower body sculpt + mobility

35–40 min
  • Split squats holding support 3 × 8 each side
  • Step-back lunges or reverse taps 3 × 10 each side
  • Dumbbell sumo squats 3 × 12
  • Glute bridge march 3 × 10 each side
  • Bird dog 3 × 10 each side

Day 4

Full body low impact

25–35 min
  • Dumbbell deadlift 3 × 10
  • Supported bent-over rows 3 × 10
  • Band tricep pressdowns 3 × 12
  • Mini-band glute finisher 3 rounds
  • 10–20 min walk or marching at home

Indian portion guide
Use these as quick anchors when logging meals.
  • 1 katori dal / rajma / chole = about 150–180 ml
  • 100 g cooked rice = about half to two-thirds of a standard bowl
  • 1 medium roti = roughly 35–40 g atta dough
  • 150 g cooked chicken/fish = about one palm and a half
  • 1 large salad bowl = at least 2 packed cups vegetables
  • 1 tbsp oil/ghee = about 120 kcal, so be careful with invisible calories
